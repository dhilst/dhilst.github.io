The only problem is have to escape '<' and '>' characters.. that are really commons
in C source files..
I was making this by hand.. ybut my codes are becoming larger.. so I make
this little perl scrip to do boring thigs to me
#!/usr/bin/perl

use warnings;
use strict;

$/ = undef;
my ($file) = @ARGV;
open (F, "<$file") || die $!;
my $str = <F>;
$str =~ s/</&�lt;/g;
$str =~ s/>/&�gt;/g;

print $str;
